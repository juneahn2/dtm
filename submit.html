Submission Guidelines

The submission deadline is July 5 , and we will send out responses on or before July 13. We look forward to a weekend of healing, inspiration, and connection! If you have any questions, please email us.

30 Minute Presentation

Please submit a 500-word proposal for this format, which can include up to three people. Proposals should include the following:

  • A clear and focused title.
  • A summary of the topic.
  • Names, preferred pronouns, short biographies, and contact information of the speaker.

7 Minute Presentation

Please submit a 200-word proposal for this format, which can include up to three people. Proposals should include the following:

  • A clear and focused title.
  • A summary of the topic.
  • Names, preferred pronouns, short biographies, and contact information of the speaker.
